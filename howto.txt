1. replace your pcsx2.exe with this pcsx2.exe
2. copy pcsx2iidxtuner.exe to your pcsx2 directory(inis folder must be in the same directory)
2. make lots of pcsx2iidxtuner.exe lnks(range 0~9)
example:
	create shortcut of pcsx2iidxtuner.exe
	in shortcut properties, add '"inis" -(a number)' next to the path
	create lots of shortcut like this so you can click them before starting pcsx2

	just click pcsx2iidxtuner.exe without any parameter to see what the hacks are & what number is assigned to
